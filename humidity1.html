Humidity (%)


It is observed that there is a larger cluster of northern hemisphere cities with high humidity (above 60% humidity). It is also worth noting that there seem to be more locations with zero humidity in the northern hemisphere than in the southern hemisphere. There are also a surprising number of locations with 100% humidity scattered across almost all latitudes.
